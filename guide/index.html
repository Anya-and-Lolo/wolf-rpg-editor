The Perfect Guide (English)

WOLF RPG Editor Complete Guide

A beginner-friendly English guide to WOLF RPG Editor, commonly called Woditor in English
and ウディタ (Udita) in Japan.

This guide walks you through making games in Woditor, from your very first event to advanced techniques.

It covers:
⋆ Things you might want to create
⋆ Common problems and how to solve them
⋆ Clear, step-by-step instructions inside the editor.

※ Note: The original Japanese guide was based on Woditor 2.x, so some screenshots and terms may differ slightly from the current English release.
