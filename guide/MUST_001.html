◆ Switching the map you’re editing

[Goal]

　Open "Sample Map A" from the Sample Game and display it
　in the main editor window.

[1]　Click the Map Selection icon.     
[2]　Switching maps

　A window like the one on the right will   
　appear. The red triangle shows which
　map is currently being edited.


　Click the map you want to open, such
　as "1: Sample Map A".
[3]　Great work!

　You have successfully switched maps!    


　※ Tip:
　　After making any changes, remember
　　to click the Save icon often so you
　　don’t lose any progress.
